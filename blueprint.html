1. What is Shopify?

Shopify is one of the most popular eCommerce platforms in the world, allowing you to easily set up an online store and sell products. Here's why Shopify is ideal for beginners:

  • Simple and beginner-friendly interface.
  • No coding knowledge required.
  • Seamless integration with suppliers and payment gateways.
  • Powerful tools to track sales, manage inventory, and grow your store.

2. What is Dropshipping?

Dropshipping is a business model where you sell products online without holding inventory. Instead, you partner with suppliers who ship products directly to your customers. Here's how it works:

  • You choose products from a supplier to sell on your Shopify store.
  • Customers place orders on your store at your selling price.
  • You pay the supplier the wholesale price, and they ship the product directly to the customer.
  • Your profit is the difference between your selling price and the supplier's cost.

3. Why Should You Start Dropshipping?

Dropshipping is one of the easiest ways to start an online business. Here are some reasons why it's perfect for beginners:

  • Low upfront costs: No need to buy inventory upfront.
  • Risk-free: Only pay for products after making a sale.
  • Scalability: Easily add more products as your business grows.
  • Flexibility: Run your business from anywhere with an internet connection.
